Chicken Biryani Recipe

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 500g chicken (cut into pieces)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 2 tablespoons Biryani masala
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 green chilies (slit)
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Whole spices (bay leaf, cinnamon, cloves, cardamom)
  • 2 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste
  • Fresh coriander leaves for garnish
  • 2 cups water

Preparation:

  • Wash the rice thoroughly and soak it for 15-20 minutes.
  • Heat oil or ghee in a pan and fry the sliced onions until golden brown. Remove half for garnish.
  • Add ginger-garlic paste, slit green chilies, and whole spices to the pan. Saut√© for a minute.
  • Add chopped tomatoes and cook until soft.
  • Add the chicken pieces, turmeric powder, red chili powder, and biryani masala. Stir well and cook for 5-10 minutes until the chicken is tender.
  • Add yogurt and cook for 5 more minutes until the chicken is cooked through.
  • Add the soaked rice and 2 cups of water. Season with salt.
  • Cover and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until rice is cooked and water is absorbed.
  • Garnish with fried onions and fresh coriander leaves.
  • Serve hot with raita or a side salad!
