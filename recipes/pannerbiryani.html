Paneer Biryani Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 cup Basmati rice
  • 200g Paneer (cubed)
  • 1 large onion (sliced)
  • 2 tomatoes (chopped)
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • Fresh coriander leaves (for garnish)
  • Salt to taste
  • 2 cups water

Instructions

  • 1. Wash and soak the basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain the water and set aside.
  • 2. In a large pan, heat oil and add cumin seeds. Once they splutter, add sliced onions and saut√© until golden brown.
  • 3. Add ginger-garlic paste and cook for a minute, then add chopped tomatoes. Cook until the tomatoes become soft.
  • 4. Add the spices (garam masala, red chili powder, turmeric powder) and cook for a minute.
  • 5. Add the paneer cubes and mix gently. Cook for 2-3 minutes.
  • 6. Add yogurt and mix well. Let the mixture simmer for 5 minutes.
  • 7. In a separate pot, bring 2 cups of water to a boil. Add the soaked rice to the boiling water with a pinch of salt. Cook until the rice is 70% done. Drain the water.
  • 8. Layer the cooked rice over the paneer mixture. Cover the pan and cook on low heat for 10-15 minutes.
  • 9. Garnish with fresh coriander leaves and serve hot with raita or salad.
